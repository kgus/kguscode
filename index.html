Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little about me.

I live in London, UK with my partner and two children. My journey into tech began with a degree in education. After searching for something that embraced both my practical side and my creative side, I was fascinated with how developing pushed me to use those skills together.

My curiosity, always on endless supply, led me to pursue web design as a career. Development gives me the freedom to problem solve, organize and create beautiful and useful sites for people. In front-end development, I get to use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is a pixel-perfect match.
